Shift Management System

A web-based platform designed to automate shift scheduling and tracking for school staff, replacing manual paper logs with a digital solution.

View Source Code

> the_problem

The school staff was relying on physical logbooks to sign in and out. This led to inaccurate time tracking, difficulty in calculating total hours worked, and frequent loss of data. The administration needed a way to digitize this process to ensure accountability and ease of reporting.

> key_features

  • Automated Logging: Staff can clock in/out with a single fingerprint scan, recording precise timestamps.
  • Admin Dashboard: Administrators can view active staff and generate weekly attendance reports.
  • Role-Based Access: Secure login system distinguishing between regular staff and admins.
  • Data Export: Ability to download logs as CSV files for processing.

> technologies_used

Java Mysql Persona digital SDK
